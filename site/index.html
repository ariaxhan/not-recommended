The point

A feed is a decision made before you arrive.

So this one removes it. You choose a question. Or take a random door out. Or close the tab. All three count as success.

Three moves. That’s it.

Less product.
More agency.

  1. 01

    Start with a question.

    Search for what you came to find instead of accepting what arrived first.

  2. 02

    Leave the loop.

    Three random Wikipedia articles offer detours with no profile and no ranking.

  3. 03

    End the session.

    Hide Shorts, sidebars, and comments. Make stopping an actual option again.
